Jitsi Online Meeting.

Full integration of Jitsi Meet for Video Meetings



Use the Odoo Calendar to create and share Video Meetings

You can create the Vide Meetings and choose the participants. The link to join the Video Meeting is mailed to each participants.

Manage Video Meetings with your participants on Calendar.

  • Option to configure location of Jitsi Meet.
  • Send Mail Invitation to Video Meeting participants.
  • Create password protected access to Video Meetings.
  • Create a link to Video Meeting on Calendar event.
  • Send an invitation to Video Meeting and password to participants by Mails.
